TYPOGRAPHY — CRITICAL Display / Heading font: "Fraunces" — a slow, optical serif with distinctive warmth and personality. Use at 700–900 weight for all H1, H2, H3 headings and stat numbers. Use at 400–500 weight for nav logo, eyebrows, and labels. Fraunces has a beautiful "wonky" optical character that looks refined and handcrafted at large display sizes — entirely unlike typical industrial web fonts. Body font: "DM Sans" — clean, refined, 300–400 weight for paragraphs, labels, captions, and form fields. DO NOT use Syne, Inter, Roboto, Space Grotesk, Poppins, or any rounded sans-serif. This is non-negotiable. Note on Fraunces: it is a mixed-case serif — do NOT force all-caps on headings. Let the natural sentence case and mixed case work. The elegance comes from the weight contrast and optical character of the letterforms, not from capitalisation. At 700–900 weight, Fraunces headlines should feel editorial and assured, not loud.
